About Our Practice

Compassionate care with a commitment to your mental health journey.

Founded in 2010, Mindful Therapy Center has been dedicated to providing exceptional psychological care to our community. Our team of licensed psychologists brings together diverse expertise and a shared commitment to evidence-based practice.

We believe in creating a safe, non-judgmental space where clients can explore their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Our approach is collaborative, empowering you to make meaningful changes in your life.

We're constantly innovating to make mental health support more accessible. That's why we're developing Kiko - an AI-powered chatbot designed to provide immediate, compassionate support to those in need of someone to talk to, available 24/7.

Kiko - Your AI Mental Health Companion

Coming Soon - Available 24/7

Kiko is our upcoming AI chatbot designed to provide immediate, compassionate support for those in need of someone to talk to. While Kiko doesn't replace professional therapy, it offers a safe space to express feelings, practice coping techniques, and get resources.

Hi, I'm Kiko. I'm here to listen and support you. How are you feeling today?
I've been feeling really anxious about work lately.
I'm sorry to hear you're feeling anxious. That's completely understandable. Would you like to talk about what's specifically worrying you, or would you prefer to try a quick relaxation exercise?
